Peppernuts

An image of a delicious bowl of peppernuts!

Peppernuts are a traditional Nordic christmas biscuit that contain different groundpsices including cinamon and cloves.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups flour
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Cup Margarine
  • 1 tsp ground cinamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves

Instructions

  1. Measure sugar and Margarine and put into a bowl.
  2. Measure the flour and pour ontop of the other ingredients.
  3. Grind up cloves in the pest and mortar.
  4. Sprinkle the spices ontop of the flour and micx the ingredients together.
  5. Break in the egg. Mix so that it is all combined. Add flour a bit at a time until the mixture is not sticky
  6. Take a small bit of the mixture and roll it itno a sausage about 6cm thick.
  7. Cut it into little pieces about 2cm wide.
  8. Place the pieces onto a baking tray.
  9. Put the tray in the oven (180 degrees) for about 8 minuets until the peppernutsare browned and starting to harden
  10. Leave to cool.
  11. Put into little plastic bags to share with friends and family.
